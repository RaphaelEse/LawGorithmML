      IIA
      118th CONGRESS
      2d Session
      H. J. RES. 98
      IN THE SENATE OF THE UNITED STATES
      
         January 16, 2024
         Received
      
      JOINT RESOLUTION
      Providing for congressional disapproval under chapter 8 of title 5, United States Code, of the rule submitted by the National Labor Relations Board relating to Standard for Determining Joint Employer Status.
   
   
      Resolved by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled
      
         
         That Congress disapproves the rule submitted by the National Labor Relations Board relating to Standard for Determining Joint Employer Status (88 Fed. Reg. 73946 (October 27, 2023)), and such rule shall have no force or effect.
      
   
   
      
         Passed the House of Representatives January 12, 2024.
         KEVIN F. MCCUMBER,
         Clerk.
